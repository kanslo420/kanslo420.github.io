Japanese Creme Sanwiches

These deep-fried sandwiches are inspired by a viral Japanese shop that had me absolutely hooked the moment I laid eyes on them. Golden, crispy but fluffy milk bread stuffed with whipped cream and strawberries or topped with custard and caramelized sugar for a crème brûlée twist— what is there not to love? This is my version of that dreamy dessert without the whole flight across the world to Japan!


PREP TIME: 20 minutes. COOK TIME:20 minutes.

TOTAL TIME:40 minutes. YEILDS:10

INGREDIENTS

    BREAD
  • 5 Japanese milk bread slices
  • vetable oil, for frying

    • WHIPPED CREME
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • CUSTARD (optional)
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cornstartch
  • 1/4 cup gradulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

    • OTHER
  • strawberries, stem removed and cut in half
  • granulated sugar

      • INSTRUCTIONS

      MAKE THE CUSTARD (OPTIONAL)

      1. In a medium saucepan, heat whole milk, vanilla extract, and salt over medium-low until just warm

      2. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch, sugar, and egg yolks until smooth.

      3. Gradually pour the warm milk into the egg mixture while whisking continuously to temper the eggs.

      4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened to a custard-like consistency.

      5. Remove from heat and stir in the butter until fully melted and incorporated.

      6. Transfer the custard to a bowl, cover the surface with plastic wrap to prevent a skin from forming, and chill in the refrigerator until completely cool

        7. MAKE THE WHIPPED CREAM

      7. In a large mixing bowl, combine heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract.

      8. Whisk the mixture until stiff peaks form.

      9. Cover the whipped cream and store it in the refrigerator until ready to use.

        10. FRY THE BREAD

      10. Heat a shallow layer of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

      11. Fry each slice of Japanese milk bread until golden and crispy on both sides, flipping carefully to prevent tearing

      12. Transfer the fried bread to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil

        13. ASSEMBLE THE SANDWICHES

        For strawberry whipped cream sandwiches:
      13. Cut each fried bread slice in half and gently open each piece to create a pocket.

      14. Fill the pocket generously with whipped cream and top with three strawberry halves.

        15. For Crème Brûlée Sandwiches:
      15. Cut each fried bread slice in half and gently open each piece to create a pocket.

      16. Fill the pocket about two-thirds of the way with whipped cream, leaving room for the custard.

      17. Spoon custard into the remaining one-third of the pocket.

      18. Sprinkle an even layer of granulated sugar over the custard and brûlée it with a kitchen torch until caramelized and golden. Allow the caramelized sugar to cool and harden slightly before serving.
